About Us
Over a Century of Banking
Having set a benchmark in banking in Sri Lanka we have set standards, created an identity and forged an unsurpassable trend. Recognised as a trend setter, we have maintained our cultural identity while providing a range of products and services. Powered by state-of-the-art technology and driven by a team of highly motivated, dynamic individuals we have become the leader in private banking in Sri Lanka.

Sustainability
At Commercial Bank, Sustainability ranks high on our agenda among our top business priorities; Sustainability has always been central to our philosophy and is entwined in our culture and in how we conduct business. We focus our efforts towards Sustainable Development under five strategic themes – i.e Financial Inclusion and Literacy, Social and Environmental Risk Assessment, Green Finance, Environment Conservation and Education.
